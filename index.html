Who We Are

Not just consultants.
Innovation partners.

Smart & Livable City Co., Ltd. (SLIC) is a Thai-born firm that bridges public institutions, private enterprises, and academic networks to co-create data-driven, citizen-centric smart cities.

We began as friends and colleagues from diverse backgrounds who shared a belief: we could offer impactful solutions at the right price point to help cities become better.

Purpose-driven

Every project starts with real community needs, not technology for its own sake.

Proof-based

Data and measurable outcomes guide every decision we make.

People-centric

Citizens are at the center of our design process.

Practical

Modular, bankable solutions that cities can actually implement.

What We Do

Innovation as a Service

Our 4C framework covers the full lifecycle of smart city development, from first conversation to lasting impact.

01

Consultation

Strategic advisory for municipalities and private developers on smart infrastructure, urban sustainability, and digital transformation.

02

Connecting

Building bridges between government agencies, universities, and the private sector to foster collaboration and scalability.

03

Capacity Building

Training smart city officers, community leaders, and local teams with the technical knowledge and creative methods they need.

04

Co-Creation

Collaborative implementation through people-public-private partnerships (4Ps), from idea generation through evaluation.

Flagship Program

Leadership for Change Cohort 16

A 9-module executive program co-organized with the Right Livelihood Foundation, designed to train practical smart city leaders who can drive real change in Thai cities.

What is LFC?

The Leadership for Change program, now in its 16th cohort, has trained over 1,500 leaders across government, private sector, and civil society. This year's theme, "Practical Smart City Leaders," focuses on the 4P framework: Purposeful, Practical, Proof-based, and People-Centric.

Who should apply?

City executives, municipal officers, smart city project managers, SE/SME/startup founders, policy designers, PPP teams, and civil society leaders who want to build real, bankable smart city projects.

Co-organized with

The Right Livelihood Foundation (มูลนิธิสัมมาชีพ), a leading Thai foundation that has championed ethical leadership and sustainable livelihoods for over 16 years. Dr. Non Akaraprasertkul serves as Course Director.
