The Brain of the Smart City.

  • Unified Data Ingestion: Collecting data from IoT, Social Media (LINE), and existing city databases.
  • AI-Powered Analysis: Automatically structuring unstructured data to find patterns and pain points.
  • UN Habitat Standard: The first platform in the region aligned with global urban indicators.

From Connectivity to Company

Before SLIC, we were friends and colleagues from diverse organizations‚Äîpublic, private, and startups. We shared a belief: we could offer impactful solutions at the right price point to help cities become better.

Originating from Thailand, our work spans Southeast Asia and connects globally to the U.S., Europe, Australia, India, East Asia, and even as far as the Solomon Islands via UN Missions. We realized that getting projects off the ground in Thailand was often stifled by process complexity, not a lack of solutions.

To solve this, we combined our expertise into a one-stop service. By coining "Innovation as a Service," we remove barriers and deliver meaningful value. Founded informally in 2022 as the Smart and Liveable City Institute for Collaboration (SLIC)‚Äîan acronym chosen to reflect our "sleek" approach‚Äîwe evolved in 2025 into a company limited. This shift empowers us to go beyond capacity building into investment, consultation, and in-house solution design, supported by hyperscaler partnerships.

We are grateful to the advisors and partners who have supported us. Special thanks to the Green Tree Foundation in Chiang Mai for incubating us as the Smart and Liveable City Institute for Collaboration. As we evolve, we remain deeply thankful for their foundational support.
