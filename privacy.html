Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Smart & Livable City Co., Ltd. ("SLIC") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tells you about your privacy rights.

2. Compliance with PDPA

This policy is strictly aligned with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019) ("PDPA") of Thailand. We collect, use, or disclose personal data only with legal justification or your explicit consent.

3. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped as follows:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone numbers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website and services (e.g., SCTHCDP).

4. How We Use Your Data

Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To register you as a new customer or partner.
  • To improve our website through analytics.
  • To comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

5. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have rights to access, rectify, delete, restrict processing, objec to processing, and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at dpo@slic.co.th.
